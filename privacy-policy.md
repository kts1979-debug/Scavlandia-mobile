# Privacy Policy for Scavlandia

Last updated: April 22, 2026

## Information We Collect

**Location:** Scavlandia uses your location to find nearby hunt stops and detect when you arrive at a location. Location data is not stored on our servers.

**Camera:** Scavlandia uses your camera to take photos at hunt stops. Photos are uploaded to complete hunt stops and saved to your account.

**Photos:** Scavlandia accesses your photo library to select images for hunt stops.

**Account Information:** We collect your name and email address when you create an account.

## How We Use Your Information

- Location is used only during an active hunt to track progress
- Photos are saved to your hunt history and displayed in your photo album
- Account information is used to save your hunt history and preferences

## Data Storage

Your hunt data and photos are stored securely using Google Firebase. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy email us at: support@scavlandia.com
