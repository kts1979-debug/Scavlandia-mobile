# Delete Your Scavlandia Account

You can delete your account directly in the app:

1. Open Scavlandia
2. Tap the **Profile** tab
3. Scroll to the bottom
4. Tap **Delete Account**
5. Confirm deletion

## What gets deleted
- Your account and login credentials
- All hunt history and photos
- Your visited locations history
- All points and stats

## Contact us
Email support@scavlandia.com and we will delete your data within 30 days.
